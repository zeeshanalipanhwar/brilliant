Rub-a-dub-dub, Find A Quick Sub

Calculus Level 1

∫e2edzln⁡(zz) \large \int_e^{2e} \dfrac{dz}{\ln(z^z) } ∫e2e​ln(zz)dz​

If the value of the integral above is in the form of ln⁡(ln⁡A+B) \ln(\ln A + B) ln(lnA+B), where AAA and BBB are non-negative integers, find A+BA+BA+B.


The answer is 3.
