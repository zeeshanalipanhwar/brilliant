2016 Digit Strings

Algebra Level 3

Following are the ten 2016-digit numbers with all their digits repeating in ascending (or increasing) order. What is the sum of the digits of the number obtained as a sum of these huge numbers?

Clarifications:

  • Despite leading with a 0, the first number has 2016 digits as well

  • Each of the numbers has a repeating part …0123456789… \dots 0123456789 \dots …0123456789…

This is simply the addition of ten 2016-digit numbers (yes, the first number on the top of the list is considered a 2016-digit as well).

By columnar addition,

The sum of digits in the 1st1^\text{st} 1st column (from the right) is 0+1+2+⋯+9=450 + 1 + 2 + \cdots + 9 = 45 0+1+2+⋯+9=45, so the carry over is c1=4c_1 = 4c1​=4 and the final digit at the end of this columnar sum is 5.

The sum of digits in the 2nd2^\text{nd} 2nd column (from the right) is 0+1+2+⋯+9=450 + 1 + 2 + \cdots + 9 = 45 0+1+2+⋯+9=45, with the addition of the carry over, c1=4c_1=4c1​=4, the final digit at the end of this columnar sum is 9, and its carry over is c2=4c_2 =4c2​=4.

The sum of digits in the 3rd3^\text{rd} 3rd column (from the right) is 0+1+2+⋯+9=450 + 1 + 2 + \cdots + 9 = 45 0+1+2+⋯+9=45, with the addition of the carry over, c2=4c_2=4c2​=4, the final digit at the end of this columnar sum is 9, and its carry over is c3=4c_3 =4c3​=4.
