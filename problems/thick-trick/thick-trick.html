G-7 Thick Trick

Geometry Level pending

A closed rectangular box is of uniform thickness xxx inches. The box has outer dimensions 6 inches, 4 inches, 3 inches and it has an inside volume of 30 cubic inches. Find xxx.


The answer is 0.5.
