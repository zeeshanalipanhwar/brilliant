Given that f(z)=z+z+z+z+z+... f(z)=\sqrt{z+\sqrt{z+\sqrt{z+\sqrt{z+\sqrt{z+...}}}}}f(z)=z+z+z+z+z+...​​​​​

Let f(z)=y f(z)=y f(z)=y and z=f−1(y) z=f^{-1}(y) z=f−1(y). Therefore y=z+y  ⟹  y2=z+y  ⟹  y2−y=z=f−1(y) y=\sqrt{z+y} \implies y^{2}=z+y \implies y^{2}-y=z=f^{-1}(y)y=z+y​⟹y2=z+y⟹y2−y=z=f−1(y)

  ⟹  f−1(y)=y2−y \implies f^{-1}(y)=y^{2}-y ⟹f−1(y)=y2−y

As for requirement y=2, therefore f−1(2)+4=22−2+4=4−2+4=6 f^{-1}(2)+4=2^{2}-2+4=4-2+4=\boxed{6} f−1(2)+4=22−2+4=4−2+4=6​
